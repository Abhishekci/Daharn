About Us

Karnataka State Police (KSP) is committed to providing professional and courteous service to all individuals, without discrimination, and in the spirit of equality. KSP strives to preserve the peace, protect life and property, detect and prevent crime, enforce the law, and safeguard citizens' rights and liberties.

Our Services

Patrol Services

We provide 24/7 patrol services to ensure the safety and security of our community.

Forensic Investigation

Our expert forensic team conducts investigations to gather and analyze evidence for criminal cases.

Surveillance Systems

We deploy advanced surveillance systems to monitor and prevent criminal activities.
